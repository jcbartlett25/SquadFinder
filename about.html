Individual Users

Looking for something to do? Your squad feed will provide you with limitless opportunities for excitement, adventure, and festivity. Join a squad and enjoy a new experience with interesting people in your area. Post squad listings so others can join you.

Squads

Find people nearby with the same interests as you. Post a squad listing and become connected with users who want to go to the same concerts, eat at the same restaurants, and see the same movies that you do.You will never need to be without a squad again.

Squadrons

Form squads with your closest friends. Private squad listings let you choose who you invite on your outings. Share exciting squad posts within your squadron and together decide where and when you want to squad up.
